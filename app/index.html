Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) - Significant untapped opportunity in a digital region

The GCC has one of the fastest-growing populations in the world. By 2020, it is estimated that the Gulf will have well over 36 million consumers and an economy worth over $2 trillion. The majority of the population will be under 25 years of age, increasingly well-educated, and all of the digital age. With smartphone penetration already higher than in the USA, demand for mobile data, applications and digital services presents significant opportunities for the new generation of digital pioneers.

Guide

Having identified a solution to a local problem, the team would approach the company, outlining the opportunity and delivering a proven process as follows:

The team would create a JV which will bring the company to the GCC. This group would ensure a detailed de-risked roll out plan is devised, validated and amended using our local knowledge and supporting verticals.

Utilising extensive local knowledge, the legal team would then help to secure all required regulatory approvals and permits to enable the solution to be marketed, sold and operated efficiently.

The generations of trading experience of the Almoayed family is brought to bear to validate offerings and to amend to ensure that the plans are fit for purpose for regional expansion.

The extensive network of contacts and businesses across the region allows a full development plan to be developed and implemented to precisely fit each market and product or service on offer.

Through our Venture Capital Platform, Almoayed would capitalize with its partnerships to ensure the roll out is fully funded and successfully reaching the desired end user.

Our local based HR team ensures that the JV is resourced with the optimum level of people and skill sets to ensure a smooth launch and growth of the organisation.
